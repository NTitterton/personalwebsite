The boat arrives upon a waiting facility for lost and shipwrecked sailors in Malaysia. There, you regale your fellow wayward souls and some of the locals of your travels. You recuperate from your journey and file paperwork to return to your Thai hometown of Trat. Talking to people who are connected to the rest of the world is a refreshing change of pace from the bizarre encounters of your journey. Unfortunately, you cannot seem to call ahead.
