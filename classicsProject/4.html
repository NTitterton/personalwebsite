You grab the sword and head back into the hut, but Polyphemus is not there. Confused, you notice a dirt path that leads deeper into the island. Following the path, you come upon Polyphemus and 5 other similarly built men.

“You should have left while you could, intruder!” Polyphemus bellows. You fight valiantly, but with the numbers stacked against you, are cut down.
